Governing Board

  • Print
  • Email

MCIEA’s Governing Board includes superintendents and teacher union presidents from Attleboro, Boston, Lowell, Revere, Somerville, and Winchester. The Governing Board is charged with establishing the Consortium’s vision, overseeing MCIEA activities, engaging the state in policy discussions, and connecting with local educators and leaders.
